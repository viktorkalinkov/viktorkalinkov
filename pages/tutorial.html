How to open doors tutorial

For this assignment we used the Fontys building for our main sours or DOORS, the camera used to film was a Canon EOS 1300D and the microphone was provided by ISSD. For inspiration we used the basic tutorial on YouTube, for example five minutes craft. The tutorial takes a look at some of the different doors at the new R10 building and the methods to open them. The group met at Fontys R10 and assigned the roles. Viktor Kalinkov did the introduction and the outro of the video. Our two subjects were Kelvin Tan and Ahmed Farah they were the people that were filmed and the ones that we had to teach how to open doors. The camera man was Aflah Al-Jabri, his task was to carry the camera and tell us when we were filming. Post production and explanation voice was done by Svetoslav Stanoev, editing was done with Adobe Premier Pro.
